Elon Musk is a billionaire entrepreneur, inventor, and business magnate known for founding or leading several high-profile technology companies. He is the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, and has been involved with other ventures such as Neuralink, The Boring Company, and OpenAI (though he is no longer associated with OpenAI). Musk is known for his ambitious vision of advancing renewable energy, space exploration, and artificial intelligence, as well as his desire to make humanity a multi-planetary species by colonizing Mars.
